My Diary
5th April 2022, Tuesday

Incredible memories to this day remain...

April 5, 2022: Unforgettable day. At 7:03 PM, I had a conversation with a girl for the first time. She said we could go together... It was a dream come true, but at the same time, my friend Mano made fun of me.

I didn't sleep that night... I couldn't. Thoughts kept swirling in my head. What if this was something more than a joke?

April 6, 2022: The next day, I couldn't stop thinking about it. My friends couldn't stop teasing me. But inside, I couldn't help but feel something special.

The days that followed were full of conversations... There was something magical about those moments, something that I never expected to experience. She said, "Let's walk together," and I didn't know what to think. I never imagined something like this could happen to me.

Mano said I was lucky... but I didn't know. I was just glad to have someone to talk to. And little by little, I learned to open up.

12th April 2022: Talked to her again, but not in class like usual. My mind was all over the place, unsure of what was going on. I still haven't figured it out...

15th April 2022: Still waiting for a response. Two days of holidays passed by, but there's still no word from her. My mind is full of questions. What should I do?

"Sometimes the heart knows what the mind cannot understand."
